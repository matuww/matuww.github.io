Oirat

ᡐᡆᡑᡆ
ᡋᡅᡒᡅᡎ

Alphabet1
1648 CE - Present1


Vertically up-to-down, left-to-right1

Languages1

Oirat Mongolian

Places1

Dzungaria, East Turkestan and Kalmykia, European Russia

Abecedary1,2

Zaya Panditga Oktorguin Dalai, an Oirat Mongolian Tibetan Buddhist monk, developed the Oirat script for writing Oirat Mongolian and transcribing Sanskrit and Tibetan.
