Article 1 of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights in Amharic

የሰው፡ልጅ፡ሁሉ፡ሲወለድ፡ነጻና፡በክብርና፡በመብትም፡እኩልነት፡ያለው፡ነው።፡
የተፈጥሮ፡ማስተዋልና፡ሕሊና፡ስላለው፡አንዱ፡ሌላውን፡በወንድማማችነት፡መንፈስ፡መመልከት፡ይገባዋል።

Transliteration

yesewi፡liji፡hulu፡sīweledi፡nets’ana፡bekibirina፡bemebitimi፡ikulineti፡yalewi፡newi።፡
yetefet’iro፡masitewalina፡ḥilīna፡silalewi፡ānidu፡lēlawini፡bewenidimamachineti፡menifesi፡memeliketi፡yigebawali።

Translation

All human beings are born free and equal in dignity and rights.
They are endowed with reason and conscience and should act towards one another in a spirit of brotherhood.
