Epigraphy3

Limestone frieze with inscription (7 ll) in Numidian; half of a bilingual inscription, the other half being Punic (1852,0305.2); instrumental in deciphering Numidian script which is alphabetic and reads from right to left like Punic; from Punic style maus (description - here)
