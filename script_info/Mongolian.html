Languages1

Mongolian

Places1

Mainly in Inner Mongolia. Also in Outer Mongolia. Formerly in territories of the Mongol Empire.

Abecedary1,4,5

Tata-tonga, a Uyghur scribe captured by Genghis Khan, adopted the Old Uyghur alphabet to write the Mongolian language and thus created the Mongolian script.
