Manchu

ᠮᠠᠨᠵᡠ
ᡥᡝᡵᡤᡝᠨ

Alphabet1
1599 CE - Present1


Vertically up-to-down, left-to-right1

Languages1

Manchu and Xibe

Places1

Manchuria and Ili region, East Turkestan

Abecedary1-6

In 1632, Dahia's reforms added diacritic marks to reduce the ambiguity faced in the original Mongolian script.
Additional letters are added for the transliteration of Chinese, Sanskrit, and Tibetan loanwords.
