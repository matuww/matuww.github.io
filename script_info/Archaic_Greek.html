Epichoric alphabets1-3

Archaic Greece was extremely decentralised, with many independent city states and their colonies scattered around the Mediterranean, using their own local or epichoric version of the Greek alphabet.

Kirchhoff (1877) grossly categorised these epichoric alphabets into four groups based on shared characteristics.
