Molodtsov

Молоԁцов анбур

Alphabet citation needed
1918 CE - 1931 CE1,2


Left-to-right citation needed

Languages1,2

Komi

Places citation needed

Northern European Russia

Abecedary1

Molodtsov Sandrö Vas'ö, also known as Vasily Aleksandrovich Molodtsov, was a Komi linguist and teacher who devised this eponymous alphabet.
