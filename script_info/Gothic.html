Alphabet1
4 c. CE - Decline in c. 6 CE1,citation needed


Left-to-rightcitation needed

Languages1

Gothic

Places1-3

Oium and Dacia (roughly modern day Ukraine, Romania, and Bulgaria), spreading to the Ostrogothic Kingdom (Italy and western Balkans) and Visigothic Kingdom (Iberia and southern France), then last attested in Crimea and north Caucasus.

Abecedary1,4

Ulfilas, a Gothic bishop and missionary of Cappadocian Greek descent, created the Gothic alphabet.
