Aethicus’ Alphabet1,2

This alphabet was found in "Cosmographia Aethici Istrici ab Hieronymo ex graeco in latinum breviarium redacta” (7/8 c. CE), which was written by Hieronimus and narrated the travels of Aethicus around the world.
Vékony (2004) described Aethicus’ Alphabet as a calligraphic and distorted version of Carpathian Basin Rovash.
