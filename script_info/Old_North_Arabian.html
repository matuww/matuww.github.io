Safaitic

Inscription on rock from al-ʿIsawi, near Namāra (southeastern Siria).3 (descriptiion and English translation - here)

Taymanitic

Inscription engraved on rock, from the area of al-Khabu al-Sharqi, some km southwest of Taymāʾ (Saudi Arabia).5 (descriptiion and English translation - here)

Hismaic

Inscription on rock found in wādī Ḥafīr, a canyon stretching for 15 km southwest of Rās an-Naqab, in the desert of Ḥismà (southern Jordan).6 (descriptiion and English translation - here)
