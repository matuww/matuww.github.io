Derived from Long-branch Younger Futhark letter Fé (ᚠ).

Old Norwegian rune poem:
Fé vældr frænda róge;
føðesk ulfr í skóge.

English translation:
Wealth is a source of discord among kinsmen;
the wolf lives in the forest.

Old Icelandic rune poem:
Fé er frænda róg
ok flæðar viti
ok grafseiðs gata
aurum fylkir.

English translation:
Wealth is the source of discord among kinsmen
and fire of the sea
and path of the serpent.

These were likely used to write on wood.
