Vagindra

Alphabet1
1905 - 1917 CE1.2


Vertically up-to-down, left-to-right1.2

Languages1,2

Buryat Mongolian and Russian

Placescition needed

Buryatia Republic, Ust-Orda Buryat Okrug, and Agin-Buryat Okrug in Siberia

Abecedary1,2

Agvan Dorzhiev, a Buryat Mongolian Tibetan Buddhist monk, developed the Vagindra script to unite the Buryats culturally.
